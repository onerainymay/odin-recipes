Earl Grey Tea Latte

The Perfect, Mellow Latte

Also known as a London Fog, this drink is soothing, fragrant, and pairs well with a pastry (or two!)

ingredients

  • 1 earl grey tea bag
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon honey (or to taste)

instructions

  1. Fill your mug up a quarter way (about 2 oz) with water and microwave for 1 minute
  2. Add the teabag and honey, stir to mix and let sit covered for 5 minutes
  3. In the meantime, add milk to a saucepan over medium heat to warm up, and whisk continuously as it warms to create froth (this can also be done in a frother or french press, if one has one)
  4. Remove the teabag and pour the milk, hold the froth to the side so you can pour it on top last
  5. Enjoy!
