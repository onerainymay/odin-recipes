15 Minute Doodh Chai

Comfort in a Mug

Heading to the kitchen and being greeted by the smell of a hot pot of chai is like a hug for the senses (yes, this is an attempt at trying to be poetic but you gotta believe me on this one). This doodh chai recipe is the same one I've grown up drinking and continue to make. The recipe is simple, but makes the perfect cup each time. Even though your chai will never be as good as my mom's, this foolproof recipe will surely yield you with the perfect drink to warm you up on a cold, rainy day!

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

  • 2 black tea bags
  • 1 cup milk
  • sugar to taste
  • optional: 4-5 cardamom pods, sprinkle of fennel seeds

Instructions

  1. Bring 1 cup of water to a simmer
  2. Add tea bags, cardamom, and fennel seeds, placing the lid back on for a few minutes
  3. Remove lid and add milk and sugar, then leave uncovered and bring to a boil
  4. Once the chai begins rising, remove from heat
  5. Use a strainer to pour into cups, enjoy!
